“The cute deflationary token with a passive yield mechanism.

Initial Supply
POM has an initial supply of 100 Million tokens. The first 50% is burned to a blackhole address.

Tasty Liquidity Pool
50% of the total supply is locked on Pancakeswap.

Uprising Price Floor
2% of every transaction is locked as liquidity in the Pancakeswap POM/BNB pool forever creating an ever rising price floor. The holders are rewarded with 2% of each transaction, the liquidity and value grows. This contributes to less volatility and a continuously increasing price floor.

Renounced Ownership
No ownership. The deployer and creator of POMTOKEN has removed himself permissions to modify the contract, giving address(0) ownership of the contract. This means that no-one can modify anything anymore.
